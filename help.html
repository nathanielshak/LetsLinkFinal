Frequently Asked Questions

What is Let's Link?
Let's Link provides an easy and fast way for organizations to connect with their constituents through technology. Our location services easily allow organizations and businesses to keep track of their members' attendance.
What technology do we use?
We use a web and mobile API called Firebase, which is provided by Google. This allows us to keep track of every organization/business that signs up with us. Every orgaization will have a custom user identification key which will make it easier for you to sign in.
What feedback forms can I use?
We offer intergration with both Google Forms and Survey Monkey to collect feedback from participants of your programs, events and gatherings etc. when they leave your location. Simply provide the URL of your existing feedback form and our app will display it to your participants. If you do not have an existing form, then sign up with Google Forms or Survey Monkey to use their simple form builder. Regardless of whether you provide a custom form, we will provide a simple rating system.
